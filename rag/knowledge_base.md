# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## Overview
AutoStream is a content automation platform designed to help creators efficiently manage and publish content across multiple platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok.

## Features
- **Multi-Platform Scheduling:** Schedule posts across different social media.
- **AI-Powered Recommendations:** Get suggestions on best times to post.
- **Analytics Dashboard:** Track your engagement metrics in one place.
- **Collaboration Tools:** Work with your team seamlessly.

## Pricing
AutoStream offers three pricing tiers:

- **Basic:** Free plan with limited scheduling and analytics.
- **Pro:** $15/month with unlimited posts and detailed analytics.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing with dedicated support and custom integrations.

## Getting Started
1. Sign up for an account on AutoStream.
2. Connect your social media platforms.
3. Create and schedule your first post.
4. Monitor your engagement via the dashboard.

## Supported Platforms
- YouTube
- Instagram
- TikTok
- Twitter
